Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

LootLizard ("we", "our", "us") operates the LootLizard mobile application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates during detecting sessions to map your hunt paths and find locations. Stored locally on your device.
  • Photos: Images you capture of your finds. Stored locally on your device with optional cloud sync.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Steps, heart rate, calories, and distance via WearOS companion app. Collected only when you connect a WearOS device.
  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account for cloud sync and social features.
  • Usage Data: App analytics to improve performance and features.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality (session tracking, find logging, maps)
  • To sync data across devices (when you enable cloud sync)
  • To power social features (friend suggestions, group hunts, sharing)
  • To display relevant ads (free tier)
  • To improve the app experience

4. Data Storage

LootLizard follows an offline-first approach. All data is stored locally on your device.

When cloud sync is enabled, data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) services.

Health data from WearOS is processed on-device and synced to your local database.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Analytics) — Google LLC
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier only)
  • Google Maps Platform — map display and geocoding

6. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Cloud data is retained while your account is active
  • You can export all your data at any time (CSV, JSON, GPX, KML)
  • Account deletion removes all cloud-stored data within 30 days

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data through the app's export features
  • Delete your account and all associated cloud data
  • Opt out of analytics in app settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads

8. Children's Privacy

LootLizard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
