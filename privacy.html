Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

LootLizard ("we", "our", "us") operates the LootLizard mobile application (the "App") for Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use and share it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. You may also sign in via Google or Apple, in which case we receive the name and email associated with that account.
  • Profile Information: Optional profile photo and bio that you choose to share with other users.
  • Find Data: Descriptions, categories, VDI readings, notes, and other metadata you enter when logging finds.
  • Photos: Images you capture or select for your finds. Photos are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud.
  • Social Content: Posts, comments, chat messages, and friend connections you create through social features.
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting us for support.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates during active detecting sessions to record hunt paths and find locations. Location is collected only while the App is in use and a session is active. You can deny location permission at any time.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Steps, heart rate, calories burned, and distance traveled via WearOS companion app. This data is collected only when you explicitly connect a WearOS device and grant Health Services permissions.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics.
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage patterns, screen views, and interaction events to help us improve the App. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Crash & Performance Data: Crash logs and performance metrics to identify and fix bugs.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email, and profile photo (if available) from the identity provider.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Core Functionality: To provide session tracking, find logging, map visualization, weather/tide data, and export features.
  • Cloud Sync: To synchronize your data across devices when you enable cloud sync.
  • Social Features: To enable friend connections, messaging, sharing, and community features.
  • Gamification: To track achievements, XP, levels, and streaks.
  • Subscriptions: To manage your subscription status and provide Pro-tier features.
  • Advertising: To display ads within the free tier of the App. Ads may be personalized based on your advertising ID unless you opt out.
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand how the App is used, identify issues, and improve features.
  • Safety & Moderation: To review reported content and enforce community guidelines.
  • Communications: To respond to support requests and send important service notifications.

4. Data Storage & Security

LootLizard follows an offline-first design. All your data is stored locally on your device first and works fully without an internet connection.

When you enable cloud sync, data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) infrastructure, which employs industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Health and fitness data from WearOS is processed on-device and stored in your local database. It is not synced to our cloud servers.

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with the following third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the App:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, Cloud Storage for photos, Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.) — Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — Advertising in the free tier. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps Platform (Google LLC) — Map display, geocoding, and location search. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple MapKit (Apple Inc.) — Map display on iOS. Apple Privacy Policy
  • NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) — Tide prediction data. No personal data is sent to NOAA.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of LootLizard, our users, or the public.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud Data: Retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated cloud data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics are retained for up to 14 months in Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash Logs: Retained for up to 90 days in Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Content Reports: Moderation records may be retained for up to 1 year for safety purposes.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Export: You can access and export all your data at any time using the App's built-in export features (CSV, JSON, GPX, KML formats).
  • Correction: You can edit your finds, sessions, and profile information directly in the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual finds, sessions, or your entire account. Account deletion removes all cloud-stored data within 30 days. To request account deletion, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the App, or email us at support@lootlizard.app.
  • Location Permissions: You can revoke location access at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to work without location, but session path tracking and find location features will be unavailable.
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics collection in Settings > Privacy within the App.
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads; iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising).
  • Push Notifications: You can manage notification preferences in the App settings or through your device settings.

8. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@lootlizard.app.

9. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on consent (account creation, location access), contractual necessity (providing App features), and legitimate interests (analytics, security).
  • Data Portability: You can export your data in machine-readable formats (JSON, CSV).
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests by contacting us.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data as described in Section 7.
  • Supervisory Authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Data is transferred to and processed in the United States via Google Cloud Platform infrastructure with appropriate safeguards.

10. Children's Privacy

LootLizard is rated for ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@lootlizard.app so we can take appropriate action.

11. Apple App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, LootLizard respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. We will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can change your tracking preference at any time in your device settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
