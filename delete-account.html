Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 8, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your LootLizard account directly from within the app:

  1. Open LootLizard and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion, your account enters a 30-day grace period. During this period:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated and you will be signed out.
  • Your profile is hidden from other users.
  • You can cancel the deletion by signing back in within 30 days.

After the 30-day grace period, the following data is permanently deleted from our servers:

Data That Is Deleted

  • Account information: Email address, display name, profile photo, handle
  • Social data: Posts, comments, reactions, friend connections, group hunt memberships
  • Direct messages: All messages sent and received
  • Cloud-synced content: Shared photos, find data uploaded to group hunts
  • Privacy settings: Block lists, notification preferences, privacy controls
  • Firebase Auth record: Your authentication credentials

Data That Is Retained

  • Local device data: Sessions, finds, and photos stored on your device are not affected by account deletion. You can clear this data by uninstalling the app.
  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained.
  • Content reports: Reports you submitted about other users' content may be retained in anonymized form for moderation purposes.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us at:
support@lootlizard.app

Please include the email address associated with your account so we can locate and process your request.
